1 — Regulatory Landscape & Classification Mandatory

Map each AI use-case to applicable Norwegian & EU regulations. Classification determines audit depth, approval gates, and evidence requirements.

PSA (Petroleumstilsynet)

  • Management Regulations §§ 4-5
  • Facilities Regulations §§ 7-11
  • Activities Regulations §§ 21-26
  • RNNP barrier management
  • MTO (Man-Technology-Org) analysis

EU AI Act

  • Risk classification (Art. 6)
  • High-risk requirements (Art. 9-15)
  • Transparency obligations (Art. 52)
  • Conformity assessment (Art. 43)
  • Post-market monitoring (Art. 61)

NORSOK / ISO

  • NORSOK Z-013 Risk assessment
  • NORSOK S-001 Technical safety
  • ISO 31000 Risk management
  • IEC 61508 Functional safety
  • DNV-RP-A203 Tech qualification

Data & Privacy

  • GDPR / Personopplysningsloven
  • Sikkerhetsloven (Security Act)
  • Sokkeldirektivet (Shelf Directive)
  • DORA (if financial data)
  • NIS2 Directive

5 — Runtime Governance & Monitoring Operational

Post-deployment monitoring ensures agents stay within qualified boundaries. Drift detection triggers re-qualification.

📡 Performance Monitoring

Real-time accuracy, latency, error rate dashboards. Alerting on degradation beyond qualified thresholds.

KPI Dashboard SLA Tracking

🎛️ Guardrails & Boundaries

Hard limits on agent actions, content filtering, scope constraints, automatic escalation to human.

🔄 Drift Detection

Monitor for model drift, data drift, concept drift. Automated alerts when agent behavior deviates from qualification baseline.
