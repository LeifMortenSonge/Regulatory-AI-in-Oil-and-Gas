📋 Feasibility

Technology novelty assessment, failure mode identification, LLM limitation mapping.

  • DNV Stage 0

Accuracy benchmarks, hallucination rates, bias testing, adversarial robustness.

  • OWASP LLM Top 10
  • Pen Test Report

📊 Explainability

Model provenance, training data lineage, decision rationale, confidence scoring.

  • EU AI Act Art. 13
  • XAI Methods

3 — Agent Qualification Pipeline

Each use-case follows a gated pipeline producing signed evidence at every stage.

Step 1

Use-Case Definition

Business objective, scope boundary, autonomy level (L0–L4), RACI.

Step 2

Risk Assessment

HAZOP for AI, consequence analysis, barrier ID, SIL determination.

Step 3

Sandbox Validation

Isolated testing, production-like data, acceptance criteria, decision recording.

Step 4

Gate Review

Multi-stakeholder sign-off: technical, regulatory, HSE, legal.

Step 5

Deployment

Shadow → supervised → autonomous. Rollback procedures documented.

4 — Audit Trail & Evidence Architecture

Every agent interaction produces immutable, timestamped evidence — defensible under PSA inspection and EU AI Act conformity assessment.

📝 Decision Logging

Full I/O capture, reasoning chains, tool calls, confidence scores. Append-only immutable store.

🔗 Evidence Chain

SHA-256 hashing, Merkle trees, RFC 3161 timestamping. Tamper-evident linking across stages.

👤 Human Override Log

Every intervention recorded with reason, linked to original agent decision for full traceability.

📦 Evidence Repository

WORM storage, versioned artifacts, retention policies aligned to regulatory requirements.

5 / 7

5 — Runtime Governance & Monitoring

Post-deployment controls ensure agents stay within qualified boundaries. Drift triggers re-qualification.

📡 Performance

Real-time accuracy, latency, error-rate dashboards. Threshold-based alerting.

🎛️ Guardrails

Hard action limits, content filtering, scope constraints, automatic human escalation, kill switch.

🔄 Drift Detection

Model, data, and concept drift monitoring. Statistical tests against qualification baseline.

🔁 Re-qualification

Scheduled + event-triggered. Model updates, data changes, or regulatory changes trigger re-assessment.
